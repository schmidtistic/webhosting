What This Is

SchmidtWorx is a personal workspace.

Some of what lives here reflects the present.
Some belongs to those who came before me.
Some is meant for those who will come after.

All of it is intentional.

Reflections

Making sense of the present.
Before it cures into hindsight.

Legacy

Writings and records created by those who came before me.

These are preserved as they were written, with context added only where it aids understanding.

This section exists to keep those voices from disappearing and to acknowledge that today’s work eventually becomes someone else’s history.
