Reflections of Kenneth Oswald Schmidt (1903-1997)

A collection of writings preserved as they were written.

Context

The majority of this was prepared for the 1995 Schmidt Family Reunion in Chicago, IL.

It is a compilation of stories, poems, reflections, songs, and family history written by Kenneth "Big Daddy" Oswald Schmidt (1903–1997).

The original document has been preserved in its entirety.

View or download the complete original document (PDF)

    • The Gift of a Letter

      (Taken from an article written by W. H. Parachin)

      When Ronald Reagan was president, he received a painful letter from a young woman in Georgia.

      Eventually, her husband committed suicide.

      Ronald Reagan’s reply conveyed compassion and understanding.

      A letter can be a gift.
